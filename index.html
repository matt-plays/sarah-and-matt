Travel & Stay

Lancaster is a beautiful destination with plenty to explore. Here are our recommendations to make your visit memorable.

🏨 Where to Stay

Lancaster Marriott at Penn Square

Downtown Lancaster

Centrally located with easy access to restaurants and shopping. Modern amenities and comfortable rooms.

Cork Factory Hotel

Downtown Lancaster

Unique boutique hotel in a historic building. Walking distance to local attractions and dining.

Fulton Steamboat Inn

Lancaster County

Distinctive hotel with steamboat-themed rooms. Family-friendly with plenty of amenities.

The Warehouse Hotel

Downtown Lancaster

Historic warehouse converted to modern hotel. Great for extended stays with a local vibe.

🍽️ Where to Eat

Central Market

Downtown Lancaster

America's oldest continuously operating farmers market. Perfect for breakfast or lunch with diverse local vendors.

The Belvedere Inn

Lancaster

Upscale dining in a Victorian mansion. Perfect for a special dinner out.

Luca

Downtown Lancaster

Contemporary American cuisine with seasonal menus. Great cocktails and atmosphere.

Shady Maple Smorgasbord

East Earl (20 min)

Massive Pennsylvania Dutch buffet. A true Lancaster County experience!

Prince Street Cafe

Downtown Lancaster

Cozy cafe with excellent coffee and pastries. Perfect breakfast spot.

🎯 Things to Do

Kitchen Kettle Village

Intercourse (20 min)

Charming village with shops, restaurants, and local crafts. Great for souvenirs and gifts.

Amish Farm Tours

Lancaster County

Experience authentic Amish culture with guided tours of working farms and countryside.

Strasburg Rail Road

Strasburg (15 min)

America's oldest operating railroad. Scenic steam train rides through Amish farmland.

Long's Park

Lancaster

Beautiful park perfect for walking, picnics, and summer concerts. Great for families.

Landis Valley Village & Farm Museum

Lancaster

Living history museum showcasing Pennsylvania German rural life and traditions.

Downtown Lancaster Arts & Shopping

Downtown Lancaster

Explore galleries, boutiques, antique shops, and the vibrant arts scene.
